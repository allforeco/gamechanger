Registration #FridaysForFuture #ClimateStrike


This is the registration page for FridaysForFuture (FFF). If you are organizing an event (strike, demonstration, Mothers Rebellion circle, Scientist Rebellion Alternative COP ...) in support for the FridaysForFuture movement, and you would like the world to know about it, you have come to the right place.

The registration has three sections:
1. This first section is about us (FFF) being able to reach you.
2. The second section (optional) is about you as a spokesperson, and media being able to reach you. (Many journalists, often activists themselves, use the FFFMap pin's email address to make direct contact to activists. This enables them to interview several countries quickly and nudge their article through the editors.)
3. The third section is about the event you are registering.

Thank you for your work!
